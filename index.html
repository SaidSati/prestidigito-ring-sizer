Perfect fit +
◆Knuckle bigger than the base? Pick the size that slides over the knuckle.
◆Time of day. Measure in the late afternoon, when warm. Fingers shrink a half size in the cold.
◆Wide bands fit tighter. For the smart ring, if between sizes, go up.
◆Confirm. The camera is a quick estimate — for a final order, cross-check with a precise method.
