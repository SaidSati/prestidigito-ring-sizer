Perfect fit +
◆Knuckle bigger than the base? Pick the size that slides over the knuckle — the ring has to get on.
◆Time of day. Measure in the late afternoon, when warm. Fingers shrink up to a half size in the cold.
◆Wide bands fit tighter. For the smart ring, if you're between two sizes, go up.
◆Confirm. Measure 2–3 times and, if you can, cross two methods. Close results = high confidence.
